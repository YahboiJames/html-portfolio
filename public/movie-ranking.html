James' Top 3 Movies of All Time

Here I will rank my top 3 movies of all time

Scott Pilgrim Vs The World

My favourite movie off all time is the cinematic adapatation of the scott pilgrim comics, directed by Edgar Wright this movie is a homage to the strugles of real people in a fantasy world and the fact that just because you are the hero doesnt mean your a good guy.

The Nightmare Before Christmas

A Tim Burton classic, the nightmare before chirstmas is a tale of discovery and spirit for doing the right thing, a stop motion animation that has aged well for its time this holiday movie can be enjoyed by all.

Hot Fuzz

Another Edgar Wright film, the second in the cornetto trilogy this british comedy takes us through a police department in a rural village uncovering a killer amongst them. A film for many laughs and quotible lines another master piece and must watch.
